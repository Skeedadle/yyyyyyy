How to use WebRetro

Background info about Roms and Emulators

What Are Roms

Roms are game programs that are stored on game cards of old game consoles.

What are emulators

Emulators are computer programs that simulate game consoles,
enabling users to play the games released for those consoles.

How To Upload Your Game

WebRetro is an emulator not a rom library so you have to upload your own game files.

Their are downloads available here

When you open WebRetro Their will be a list of systems choose the console (That is in parentheses), click the console that your game is from
Then Their will be a choose file button select that button then select your game

Controls

You can set your keybinds by pressing the controls button > KeyBinds
